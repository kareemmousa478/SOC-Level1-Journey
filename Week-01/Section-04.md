# Section 04 - Cyber Defence Frameworks

## Overview

This section introduced the most common cybersecurity frameworks used to understand, classify, and respond to cyber attacks.

---

## Topics Covered

- Pyramid of Pain
- Cyber Kill Chain
- Unified Kill Chain
- MITRE ATT&CK Framework

---

## Key Concepts

### Pyramid of Pain

A model that explains how difficult it is for attackers to change different indicators after being detected.

### Cyber Kill Chain

A framework describing the stages of a cyber attack from reconnaissance to actions on objectives.

### Unified Kill Chain

An extended version of the Cyber Kill Chain that covers modern attack techniques.

### MITRE ATT&CK

A knowledge base of adversary tactics and techniques used by security teams for threat detection and analysis.
